Who We Are

SHABAN-MD ek modern WhatsApp bot project hai jo automation ko simple aur reliable banata hai. Hamara focus developer-friendly experience, security, aur day-to-day tasks ko asaan banana hai — bina complicated setup ke.

What is SHABAN-MD?

SHABAN-MD ek Baileys (WhatsApp Web reverse-engineered library) par based bot framework hai. Is se aap moderation, utilities, media tools, auto-responses, aur custom workflows jaisi features enable kar sakte hain.

Key Features

  • Fast & stable connection using Baileys
  • Modular commands & easy configuration
  • Auto-reply, media tools, group utilities
  • Deploy-friendly (cloud/VPS/desktop)

Safety & Compliance

  • Policy-aligned usage: spam, abuse, ya unauthorized actions avoid
  • Account safety best-practices (rate limits, human-like interaction)
  • Users ke rights & consent ka khayal (see our Privacy Policy)
  • Brand/trademark aur platform rules ka ehtiram
